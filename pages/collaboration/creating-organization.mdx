### Organization overview 

An organization in Firecamp represents a centralized place where teams can collaborate, manage APIs, and streamline their development process. The organization acts as a container for multiple workspaces, making it easy to organize and manage your API collections across teams.

In this guide, you'll learn about the following operations:
- #### [Creating organization](/collaboration/creating-organization#1-creating-organization-1)
- #### [Switch between organization](/collaboration/creating-organization#2-how-to-switch-between-organization)


 #### 1. Creating organization

1. Navigate and click ` Create ` dropdown in the upper right side. 
2. Click `New organization`
3. A popup will open, enter your `organization name` and click `create`
4. Once you click `create`, popup will open and ask you to switch to a new organization. 
5. By clicking `Yes switch to the new workspace` it'll redirect you to `Main workspace` that is the default workspace of the organization that was just created.

<Callout type="info">
Firecamp creates a default workspace called 'Main Workspace,' which you can later update the name of.
</Callout>

#### 2. Switch between organization 

1. Navigate and click ` Create ` dropdown in the upper right side. 
2. Click `Switch organization`. 
3. A popup will open that shows the list of organizations you're a part of. 
4. Click your required organization and it'll redirect you to the selected organization.
